About

A career dedicated to advancing paediatric outcomes and delivering exceptional care for children with endocrine and complex conditions.

I currently serve as Professor and Chair of Paediatrics & Child Health at the University of Newcastle and work as a Paediatric Endocrinologist at John Hunter Children's Hospital.

I am an innovative educator recognised with multiple teaching awards, now actively pioneering the integration of artificial intelligence into medical education. My leadership extends to co-leading engagement and technical initiatives within the International Paediatric Academic Leaders Association (IPALA) and driving statewide paediatric trainee research education.
